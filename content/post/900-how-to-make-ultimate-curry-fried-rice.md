##### The ingredients needed to make Curry Fried Rice:

1. Get  Eggs *lightly whisked
1. Prepare  Canola Oil *and extra if needed
1. Make ready  Onion OR 2 Spring Onions
1. Get  Ham, Sausages, Chicken, Pork, Beef *cut into small pieces
1. Prepare  Peas OR chopped Green Capsicum
1. Make ready  Garlic *grated
1. Prepare  Curry Powder
1. Make ready  Salt
1. Make ready  servings Cooked Rice *about 360g, warmed
1. Take  Soy Sauce




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Curry Fried Rice:

1. Combine all seasonings in a small bowl. If you use cold left-over cooked rice, warm it in the microwave.
1. Heat Oil in a frying pan or wok over medium high heat, cook Egg and transfer to a plate.
1. Add extra Oil if required, cook Onion, Ham (OR Sausages, Chicken, Pork, Beef) and Peas (OR chopped Green Capsicum) until cooked through.
1. Return the cooked Egg, add Garlic, Curry Powder and Salt, mix well, then add warmed rice and stir well to combine.
1. Finally drizzle over Soy Sauce, toss and stir until aromatic.
