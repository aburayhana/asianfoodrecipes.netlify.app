##### The ingredients needed to make Asian Tortilla:

1. Prepare 1/2 pcs cucumber
1. Get 1 pcs carrot
1. Get 1 roll egg
1. Prepare 3 pcs crab stick
1. Make ready 3 spoon bbq sauce
1. Make ready 1/4 enoki (stirfry with garlic and oyster sauce)
